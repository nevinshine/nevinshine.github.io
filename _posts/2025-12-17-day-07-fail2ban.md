# 🔫 The Sniper is Live

Today, I moved from passive defense to active defense. I installed **Fail2Ban** to automatically detect and ban IPs that try to guess my SSH password.

## 🛠️ What I Built
* **Monitor:** Watches `/var/log/auth.log`.
* **Trigger:** 3 failed attempts = Permanent Ban.
* **Firewall:** Automatically updates UFW rules.

## 🧪 Verification
I manually banned a test IP (`1.2.3.4`) and verified it was blocked in the firewall.

> "Security automation is the only way to scale."
